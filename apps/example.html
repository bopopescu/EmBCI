Your README is a good place for users to know about your project.

Writing README also helps you to make it clear about these two key questions:

  • What problem does your application solve? / What service will it provide?

  • What’s your plan/roadmap to achieve the goal step by step?

About¶

This is just an example app using EmBCI WebUI framework.

How to use¶
